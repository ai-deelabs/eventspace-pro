The studio

An event production studio with the craft of a workshop.

EventSpace Pro was founded in 2009 by a stagehand and an industrial designer who were tired of brittle booth rentals that fell apart by day two. Fifteen years and 1,200+ events later, we still build like we're putting our name on every joint.

Our 18,000 sq ft Brooklyn workshop houses carpenters, welders, electricians, designers, and a production team that travels nationwide. We design and build everything in-house — no outsourcing, no compromises.

In-house fabrication

Carpenters, welders, and electricians under one roof.

Fully insured

$5M general liability, workers' comp, equipment cover.

Nationwide reach

Dedicated transport in our own fleet across 12 cities.

Reusable systems

Modular components reduce waste and budget by 30%.
